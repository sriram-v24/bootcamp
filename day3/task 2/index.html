iphone 15 pro

The *iPhone 15 Pro* features a *titanium body, making it lighter and more durable. It is powered by the **A17 Pro chip, offering enhanced performance and efficiency. The device includes a **48MP main camera* with improved low-light capabilities. A new *Action Button* replaces the mute switch for customizable functions. It also features a *USB-C port*, replacing Lightning for faster data transfer and charging.
